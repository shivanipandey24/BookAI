About the Developer

Hi! I'm SHIVANI PANDEY, a Computer Science student passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and software development.

As someone who genuinely enjoys reading books, I often found it difficult to discover new books after finishing one I loved. That inspired me to build BookAI, a machine learning-powered recommendation system designed to help readers discover books similar to their interests.

This project combines two of my passions: reading and technology. Using collaborative filtering, similarity-based recommendations, Flask, and Python, I created a platform that helps users find their next favorite book.
